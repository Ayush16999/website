Overview

Invicta shipping is founded by marine enthusiasts who want to streamline the underline issues faced. They intend to provide one stop solution ensuring delivery of superior services to its partner. It is there innate knowledge of the operations and high performing unified crew that makes them unique. Invicta Shipping enages in all services like Crew Manning, Ship Store Supplies, Shore Technical Assistance/Services ( viz. Pre-vetting, Pre-purchase, Pre-CDI etc), Trainings (Non D.G approved) and HR Services (Policy Creation and implementation).

Human resources (HR) is the department within a business that is responsible for all things worker-related. That includes recruiting, vetting, selecting, hiring, onboarding, training, promoting, paying, and firing employees and independent contractors.
